Vietnamese Pork and Shrimp Spring Rolls (Gỏi Cuốn)

Ingredients:

Instructions:

  1. Cook rice vermicelli according to package instructions. Drain, rinse, and set aside.
  2. Boil pork belly for about 20 minutes until cooked through. Cool and slice thinly.
  3. Cook shrimp until pink and opaque. Cool, peel, and slice in half lengthwise.
  4. Wash and dry all herbs and vegetables.
  5. Dip a rice paper wrapper in warm water briefly, then lay on a damp work surface.
  6. Layer shrimp, pork, herbs, noodles, and lettuce on the wrapper.
  7. Roll tightly, folding in the sides as you go.
  8. Repeat with remaining ingredients.

Peanut Dipping Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup hoisin sauce
  • 1/4 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp lime juice

Mix all ingredients in a saucepan over medium heat until smooth and warm.

Chef's Tips:

  • For best results, use fresh ingredients and serve immediately.
  • Don't overstuff the rolls to ensure easy rolling and eating.
  • Experiment with different herbs and vegetables for variety.
