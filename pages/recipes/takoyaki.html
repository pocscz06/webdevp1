Instructions:

  1. Prepare the batter: In a large bowl, mix flour, dashi stock, beaten eggs, soy sauce, and salt until smooth.
  2. Heat the takoyaki pan over medium heat. Generously oil each mold and the surface of the pan.
  3. Fill each mold with batter, slightly overflowing.
  4. Add fillings: Place a piece of octopus, some green onion, pickled ginger, and tempura scraps into each mold.
  5. Cook and rotate: As the takoyaki starts to cook, use a pick or skewer to rotate each ball. Cook until golden brown and crispy on the outside.
  6. Serve: Transfer the takoyaki to a plate and drizzle with takoyaki sauce and Japanese mayonnaise. Sprinkle with bonito flakes and aonori.
  7. Enjoy! Serve immediately and enjoy your homemade takoyaki.

Tips & Variations:

  • Use a variety of fillings like shrimp or scallops for different flavors.
  • Experiment with different sauces, such as sriracha mayonnaise or ponzu.
  • For an extra crispy exterior, add a small amount of cornstarch to the batter.
