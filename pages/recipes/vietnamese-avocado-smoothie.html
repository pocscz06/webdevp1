Vietnamese Avocado Smoothie (Sinh To Bo)

Ingredients:

Instructions:

  1. Cut the avocados in half, remove the pit, and scoop out the flesh.
  2. In a blender, combine the avocado flesh, milk, condensed milk, and ice cubes.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy, about 30-60 seconds.
  4. Taste and adjust sweetness by adding more condensed milk if desired.
  5. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.
  6. Optionally, garnish with a sprinkle of cocoa powder or a drizzle of condensed milk.

Chef's Tips:

  • For the best flavor, use ripe Hass avocados.
  • For a dairy-free version, use coconut milk instead of regular milk.
  • To make it extra creamy, freeze the avocado chunks for an hour before blending.
  • For a thicker smoothie, use less milk or add more ice.
